视频快速预览




功能介绍

通过快速预览来寻找自己想要的视频
Find the video you want through quick preview

选择一个或多个目录，App会帮你列出目录下所有的视频文件。
Select one or more directories, and the App will help you list all the video files in the directory.

鼠标划过视频，你就可以快速预览整个视频。
Move the mouse over the video, and you can quickly preview the entire video.

你可以通过它来快速寻找素材，快速锁定某个镜头。
You can use it to quickly find materials and quickly lock a certain shot.

如果用来搜寻监控录像中的事件，那是再好不过了。
If you use it to search for events in surveillance videos, it is perfect.
