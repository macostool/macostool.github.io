扫码录入




功能介绍

现在可以使用Mac来扫码录入了
你可以用Mac电脑来做扫码入库，销售，库存盘点等操作
Now you can use Mac to scan and enter. You can use Mac to scan and enter, sell, inventory and other operations.

你可以内置的相机或是连接iPhone，还可以连接其他任何相机。
You can use the built-in camera or connect to iPhone, or any other camera.

可以识别市面上大部分的条码和二维码。
It can recognize most barcodes and QR codes on the market.

你可以选择将识别结果自动放到剪切板，或立即粘贴到任意App的某个文字框中。
You can choose to automatically put the recognition results into the clipboard, or paste them immediately into a text box in any App.

你可以将窗口最小化到后台，它仍然可以继续工作。
You can minimize the window to the background, and it will still continue to work.

访问相机只是为了扫码，App没有连网权限，我们不会收集您的任何信息。
“立即粘贴”功能需要开启相关辅助功能权限。
Accessing the camera is only for scanning, the App does not have Internet access, and we will not collect any of your information. The "Paste Now" function requires the relevant accessibility permissions to be enabled.
